What is NSS IIT Patna all about??

By NSS Team

18 July 2018

share:

NSS stands for National Service Scheme.It is an organisation that organises various socially productive events that are concerned with the growing needs of the environment,the people,the children and all those who form a part of our spaces ,the centre of activity being Amhara village.It has emerged as one of the largest and well developed body that aims to create a sense of "Simplicity of Life and diginity of Labour".

NSS mainly comprises of the student body including mentors. NSS IIT Patna is headed by Sir Rajgopal Rao and all the activities are conducted under his guidance and support. The body of NSS consists of various cells in which volunteers execute different actions . The main Cells are: Teaching,Technical Skills,Rural Development,Environment,Chetna. Various cells function on different basis. Teaching group conducts regular classes and weekly tests for the children of Amhara village ,technical skills group helps them to upgrade their knowledge on computers so taht they feel comfortable with the world.Environment group conducts activities like plantation drives,cleanliness drives,etc.The Chetna and Rural Development are concerned with the youth of the society helping them to find better ways of finding job opportunities. It also helps to spread information regarding various government schemes and organisations.

Apart from this there are various events organised depending on the ocassions. Events are specially conducted on national holidays and on birthdays of our nation leaders.This not only spreads information but also motivates others to contribute their parts to the society. Since NSS IIT Patna has come into existence,it has been setting various goals and also accomplishing it sucessfully. Various events are organised in the Amhara village among children of all age groups to discover the hidden talent in them.




Whom do we serve??

We serve the people of some selected villages near the campus these include Amhara village,Raghavpur village and some more. Various teams of IITP are working to bring light in the future of students living there,to empower the woman of village and to make those villages like father of our nation want them to be. Apart from these we also conduct various activities both inside and outside the campus. In future we are also planning to extend our scope and include some more village.
